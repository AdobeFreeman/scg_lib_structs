10x Chromium Single Cell 3' Solution v2

The Chromium Single Cell 3’ Solution v2 chemistry is shown here. Oligo sequence information is taken from The 10x Genomics Technical Note. If you have Cell Ranger, you can find out all the cell barcodes in (using Cell Ranger v2.1.0 as an example) /path/to/cellranger-2.1.0/cellranger-cs/2.1.0/tenkit/lib/python/tenkit/barcodes



Adapter and primer sequences:

Beads-oligo-dT: |--5'- CTACACGACGCTCTTCCGATCT[16-bp cell barcode][10-bp UMI](T)30VN -3'

Template Switching Oligo (TSO): 5'- AAGCAGTGGTATCAACGCAGAGTACATrGrGrG -3'

ISPCR: 5′- AAGCAGTGGTATCAACGCAGAGTACAT -3′

Illumina Truseq Read 1 primer: 5'- TCTTTCCCTACACGACGCTCTTCCGATCT -3'

Illumina Truseq Read 2 primer: 5'- GTGACTGGAGTTCAGACGTGTGCTCTTCCGATCT -3'

Truseq adapter (double stranded DNA with a T overhang):


        5'-  GATCGGAAGAGCACACGTCTGAACTCCAGTCAC -3'
        3'- TCTAGCCTTCTCG -5'

Library PCR primer 1: 5'- AATGATACGGCGACCACCGAGATCTACACTCTTTCCCTACACGACGCTC -3'

Library PCR primer 2: 5'- CAAGCAGAAGACGGCATACGAGAT[8-bp sample index]GTGACTGGAGTTCAGACGTGT -3'

Sample index sequencing primer: 5'- AGATCGGAAGAGCACACGTCTGAACTCCAGTCAC -3'

Illumina P5 adapter: 5'- AATGATACGGCGACCACCGAGATCTACAC -3'

Illumina P7 adapter: 5'- CAAGCAGAAGACGGCATACGAGAT -3'
